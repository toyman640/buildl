Frequently Asked Questions

Posting a project on Buidl is easy! Simply sign up for a founder account, then navigate to the "Post a Project" section on our platform. Fill out the project details, including project description, requirements, budget, and timeline. Once submitted, your project will be visible to our community of creators.

Buidl accommodates a wide range of projects across various industries and sectors. Whether you're building a web2 application, exploring the potential of web3 technologies, or seeking expertise in AI, blockchain, design, or marketing, our platform provides the resources and talent you need to bring your vision to life.

Buidl offers tools and features to help you evaluate and select the right creators for your project. Review creators' profiles, portfolios, and ratings to assess their skills and expertise.

At Buidl, we prioritize quality and security in every aspect of our platform. We implement robust verification processes for creators, ensuring that only qualified professionals join our community. We also provide project management tools, secure payment options, and dispute resolution mechanisms to safeguard your project's success.

Buidl is committed to supporting founders throughout their journey. Beyond project completion, we offer resources, guidance, and networking opportunities to help founders grow their ventures and succeed in the digital economy. Whether you're looking for post-project support, mentorship, or strategic advice, Buidl has you covered.

Have a question that is not answered? You can contact us at support.
